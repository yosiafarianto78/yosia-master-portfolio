Who is Yosia?

What drives someone to constantly seek better ways to solve problems and improve processes? For me, it’s a passion for innovation and a willingness to grow. I love working with new technologies and using IT to turn manual, repetitive tasks into efficient, automated solutions. Currently, I work on improving workflows and making every part of a process as effective as possible.


I graduated from Calvin Institute of Technology, where I not only gained a strong foundation in Computer Science but also studied Theology. This unique combination taught me to approach challenges with both technical expertise and a deeper sense of purpose. I believe in doing things with care and always embrace new challenges as opportunities to grow.


Coming from a small village in West Kalimantan called Sentebang, I learned the value of hard work, humility, and community. These principles guide me every day. The idea of "Ora et Labora" (Pray and Work) keeps me focused, while "Nil Sine Numine" (Nothing Without Divine Will) reminds me to stay humble and purposeful in everything I do.


I enjoy learning from every experience, value teamwork, and love connecting with people from different backgrounds. With a good sense of humor and strong communication skills, I build great relationships and create a positive work environment. Whether it’s refining processes or finding smarter ways to handle tasks, I’m always eager to contribute to meaningful progress.
